Synopsis

Charade is a 1963 film directed by Stanley Donen and starring the dynamic duo of Audrey Hepburn and Cary Grant. Set against the backdrop of the enchanting Parisian streets, this film weaves a tale of suspense, romance, and humor that has left an indelible mark on the world of cinema.
