Meet our Founders

Jacob and Emily first met in a small San Francisco café, where a shared love for coffee sparked both conversation and connection. From exploring the city’s coffee culture to dreaming about creating a place of their own, they were inspired by the way a single cup could bring people together.

Years later, their journey led them to Harrisburg, Pennsylvania, where they turned that dream into Brews & Bonds. Blending West Coast inspiration with local charm, their shop quickly became a cozy hub for connection and community. Today, every cup they brew celebrates their story — a blend of love, passion, and the belief that coffee is best enjoyed when it brings people closer together.
