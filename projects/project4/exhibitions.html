EXHIBITIONS ABOUT VISIT ARTISTS & EVENTS

WHAT WE
SHOWCASE

From groundbreaking contemporary installations to timeless works on canvas, the Aurora Art Museum curates exhibitions that highlight both established and emerging artists. With rotating exhibits, interactive installations, and a permanent collection featuring over 2,000 pieces, every visit offers something new to explore.

A PLACE TO
EXPLORE

Located in the heart of downtown, the museum’s light-filled galleries, sculpture garden, and café offer visitors a welcoming environment to slow down, reflect, and engage. Our goal is not only to exhibit art but to create meaningful experiences that stay with you long after you leave.

CURRENT EXHIBITIONS

Atmospheres of Nature

Paintings and works on paper that explore landscape, mood, and place, focusing on how artists translate the natural world into emotional and immersive environments.

Portraits & Presence

This exhibition centers on the human figure, examining observation, identity, and personal connection through large-scale portraiture and contemporary figurative work.

UPCOMING EXHIBITIONS

Drama in Motion

Dynamic scenes and expressive compositions capture moments of tension, movement, and storytelling, highlighting the power of gesture and contrast.

Narratives of the City

Inspired by daily life and shared experiences, these artworks blend culture, history, and visual storytelling to reflect the rhythm of modern urban spaces.

PAST HIGHLIGHTS

Illuminated Forms

Artists experiment with material, scale, and spatial design to create immersive works that shift perception and invite viewers to engage with light, form, and space.

Local Visions

Featuring work by regional artists, this exhibition celebrates creative voices shaping the local art scene through painting, sculpture, photography, and mixed media.
